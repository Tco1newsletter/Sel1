Relationship Building

I Can be a Good Friend

I can be a good Friend Activity was conducted under Relationship theme. Students learnt the importance of friendship and various qualities which we need to inculcate in ourselves to be a good friend.



Anger Management

Children were taught the most important skill required to become a matured being. The class was conducted in a very interactive way. We showed a movie, then an information session was delivered for better understanding. Finally a worksheet was given to children to check the learning outcome. We were glad to see so many positive responses from our children.



My Definition of a Family

My definition of a family activity was conducted under relationship skill theme . The main aim was to make our learners understand the importance of our strongest pillar i.e. our family . They also learnt that their friends, pets, house helps can also be a part of their family.





Social - Awareness

Empathy (Kindness to Environment)

Students created a beautiful “Bird Feeder” for showing respect, kindness and love towards their feathery friends. They learnt how one should be empathetic towards not only people but also towards our environment and nature.

Class 4 afternoon

In SEL session first students played a word ladder game separately and then in teams. Then teacher asked them why they scored better while playing in teams and why team work is important in our life.This session helped them to understand the importance of team work in everyone’s life.

Class 5 afternoon

Interest Inventory form was shared with the students and students were asked to fill in the blanks with their favourites and share it with their teacher. A game was played in the class wherein teacher shared three answers from one student’s interest inventory and gave students a chance to think who you are talking about.This activity helped in strengthening bonds between the students and helped them to get along with each other in a better way.
